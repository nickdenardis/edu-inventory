Success Story - Randy Davis and John Jenkins

Randy Davis (left) graduated from Lane's Exercise and Movement Science program. John Jenkins (right), graduated from Lane's EMT Program. They own and operate ProFitness Personal Training in downtown Eugene.

In 2009, they squeezed refurbished gym equipment into a small downtown Eugene space, and launched ProFitness Personal Training. A devoted membership grew, and with it, the need for more room. In February 2012, ProFitness relocated to a larger building a half-block from the Hult Center.

Giving from the heart with a charitable remainder trust

Jane King wants to leave a legacy at Lane Community College. In 2004 she created an endowment to do just that. The endowment will fund the kinds of unique programs at Lane that she’s found most meaningful.

It was Jane King's two children, Jennifer and Rob, and her husband, Don, who first encouraged her to set up an endowment for the Lane Community College Foundation to fund innovative programs at Lane.

Small Business Management Program Helps a Local Business With Growing Pains

The first few years Lane Community College alums Zach Erdmann and Lyndsey Billington operated their fledgling business from home, things went smoothly.

Their Eugene-based company, Premium Efficiency, conducts energy audits and performs air duct, air-sealing and insulation services for residential properties.
