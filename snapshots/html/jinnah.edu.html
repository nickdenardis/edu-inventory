Mohammad Ali Jinnah University

Mohammad Ali Jinnah University was established in 1998 after the grant of a charter by the Government of Sindh. The University, unlike other institutions which have a degree awarding status only, enjoys the privileged status of a comprehensive university. The objective in naming the University after Quaid-e-Azam was to offer the highest quality of education and research.  Read more
