Basic Sciences

Your first five semesters at MUA will establish your understanding of health, disease and what medicine can begin to accomplish—plus preparation for the U.S. Medical Licensing Exam (USMLE).

Read More

Clinical Education

In your clinical semesters, you will leave the MUA campus and spend 72 weeks in clinical clerkships at teaching hospitals in the U.S. and Canada.

Student Life

MUA is located on Nevis, a beautiful and historic Caribbean nation (just 200 miles from Puerto Rico) that provides an ideal environment for studying and—when time allows—relaxing.

Read More

Residency Placement

MUA students obtain residencies in leading medical centers across the U.S. and Canada. A large number of our graduates are selected as Chief Residents and pursue sub-specialty fellowships.
