Center for Community-Based Learning's name
is changing to Institute for Community
Engagement and Scholarship (ICES)

The new name was selected to reflect the evolvement of CCBL into an institute focusing on faculty innovation in teaching combined with research and service while still retaining focus on community outreach.

The change will be effective July 1 and you can read all about it here!

First Oral Health Care program in the
Nation!

The Master of Science: Oral Health Care Practitioner (MS:OHCP) program, the first approved program in the nation to prepare advanced dental therapists, provides an opportunity for licensed dental hygienists to advance their education and serve the community. Learn more...

Introducing the Criminal Justice Master of Science degree

Starting in the fall of 2011, this new degree, designed for the criminal justice working professional, will provide the advanced knowledge and skills needed to be an effective, ethical leader in criminal justice organizations. Combining online and traditional classroom instruction, you will learn from faculty who are recognized and respected leaders in their field. Learn more!
