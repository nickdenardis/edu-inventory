EMCC News

 

  • Scooba campus holds Awards Day program

    East Mississippi Community College recognized outstanding students April 26 at its Scooba campus during the annual Awards Day program. More than 75 individual awards were handed out, as well as recognitions for service to EMCC and membership in honors organizations.

  • LINK award winner celebrates Sweet 16

    This year is Sweet 16 for Gina Thompson. The chair of the Fine Arts and Humanities Division at EMCC's Golden Triangle campus, celebrating her 16th anniversary with the college, has received the Columbus-Lowndes Development LINKs Community College Educator of the Year award.

  • Project IMPACT Clay County enters Phase 2

    Industry employers arent wasting their time with unqualified job-seekers, so EMCC's Project IMPACT Clay County is focusing on only the most essential skills. IMPACT, the Integrating Manufacturing Pathways And Career Training program, is under way at East Mississippi Community College's West Point-Clay County Center.

  • Read the spring 2012 edition of The Lions' Pride

    The Lions' Pride is East Mississippi Community College's alumni magazine. Visit this link to read the spring 2012 edition ... lots and lots of good news from Lion Country!

Upcoming Event

 
May 11-12: Three EMCC graduations... Registrar's office closed May 10-11

East Mississippi Community College will hold graduation ceremonies May 11-12 for its summer 2011, fall 2011 and spring 2012 graduates. The registrar's office will be closed May 10-11 as the staff processes final grades, but will re-open on May 14 for students who want to register.
 
For more details:
  • Visit EMCC Academic Calendar
  • Call the registrar's office at (662)
    243-1920 or (662) 476-5040
