First National University through its embraced universities and colleges offers Open Enrollment to all who wish to learn natural therapeutics be it homeopathy, naturopathy, alternative medicine, or drugless osteopathy. Students may remain in open enrollment status until such time as they elect to pursue a degree. Note, however, that the Oriental Medicine program is a degree only program.

At that time students may petition the University to be accepted into the degree program of their choice, and once accepted courses taken in open enrollment will be applied to their degree program.

The University has transitioned from a "brick and mortar" program to internet distance learning limited residency programs in order to better serve its American and World-wide student body. Our goal is to offer the best courses available for natural medicine and to aid practitioners of other branches of the healing arts to truly transition as competent practitioners of Oriental Medicine, Naturopathy, Natural Medicine and Alternative Medicine.

As opposed to treating symptoms and suppressing problems, we are proud to offer programs in Oriental and Western natural medicine for those interested in truly learning how to heal the sick. As Samuel Hahnemann stated, "the high and only calling of the Physician is to heal the sick, that is to say, to cure."

If you feel that you have the calling to be a healer of your fellow man we invite you to begin your journey by enrolling in our Open Enrollment program.
