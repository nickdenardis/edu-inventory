Our Mission!

Our Mission at Mid-Cities Barber College is to provide a complete training program that allows the students to obtain the necessary skills and successfully pass the Texas licensure examination. We believe through our program that our students will be completely prepared and accomplished leaders within the Community and Barbering Industry.
