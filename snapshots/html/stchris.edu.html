Welcome to our Homepage

| |


University El Hadji Ibrahima Niasse (UEIN) is chartered since February 2000 with Ref 000809/AG/MEN/DES by the Ministry of Education, Senegaland is permanently recognized by the Government of Republic of Senegal, to confer MD Degree to students who are eligible to practice medicine in the country.

Saint Christopher Iba Mar Diop (SCIMD) College of Medicine, under the university UEIN, has a main campus located in Dakar, Senegal, offering courses in Frenchfrom 2003 andhas a continual growing population ofmedical students. The student population isobtained from 21 different countries (Benin, Burkina Faso, Cameroon, and Central Republic of Africa, Comoros, Congo, Cte dIvoire, Djibouti, France, Gabon, Guinea Conakry, Guinea Bissau, Lebanon, Mali, Morocco, Mauritania, Senegal, Chad, Togo, Tunisia, and USA).

SCIMD College of Medicineis planning to run satellite sub-campusesin Africa and other continents, similar to the one in Dakar, Senegal.

Courses offered at SCIMD College of Medicine are at the forefront of academic knowledge and are taught by enthusiastic and encouraging faculty that creates an excellent environment of learning. Our main focus is to provide quality education, which will help students to achieve basic and advanced medical knowledge to develop their insights into clinical practice.

An English programhas been introduced at the main campus in Senegal from January 2008.
