Hit the ground running!
If you're beginning post-secondary studies at Nipissing in the fall, your first step is to attend New Student Orientation (NSO) in July where you'll meet other students in your program and learn what it means and what it takes to be successful! Click here for dates and details on our NSO program.
Nipissing students receive $2 million in awards
This year, Nipissing will distribute about 1,500 separate scholarships, bursaries and awards in order to assist students with the rising costs of living. A huge thank you goes out to all of our donors who made these awards possible and who have contributed significantly to ensuring the success of our students. View the full story and image gallery here.

Re-connecting to learning
Nipissing offers an individualized first-year orientation program designed specifically to assist mature and transfer students with the transition to post-secondary studies. Click here for more information about our NST program.
30% off tuition for full-time undergrads
On January 5, 2012, the Ontario government announced that it would provide 30% off tuition for full-time undergraduate students in Ontario who meet the eligibility requirements. Click here to find out more.

Our Education degree, your path to success
Nipissing offers Concurrent Education programs in conjunction with several Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Science degrees, including English Studies, Geography, History, Mathematics, Computer Science and Psychology. Click here for more information on our Concurrent BEd programs.
There's something for everyone
