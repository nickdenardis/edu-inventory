Changing lives, changing communities, changing the world.

Ministry Preparation
José Claudio has been actively involved in ministry at his local church for many years, however he says that his experience at Colgate Rochester Crozer Divinity School has given him the opportunity to learn about social justice issues and his own denominational social principles. He says, “Now I’m passing that knowledge on to the local congregation and other people in the surrounding community. I believe that this is one reason why students come to CRCDS - to gain knowledge and pass it on to others.”
  • José Claudio, M.Div. Student
