“Mr. Stand and Deliver”, Edward James Olmos, is doing it again- this time he’s standing and delivering for adult education! Because of the impact that adult education has had on his life, and the lives of his family and friends, Mr. Olmos gladly offered to work with supporters of adult education to produce a promotional public service announcement (psa) to generate state-wide support for our cause.  Adult school administrators attending the CAEAA conference each received copies of this psa to use in their districts.   If you would like to view the video, click on the link provided below: http://www.ccaestate.org/olmosVideo.html
