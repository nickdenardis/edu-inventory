Natal Chart Rectification

Webinar By Lana Kirtser

Largely out of necessity, astrologer Lana Kirtser journeyed into the terra incognita of astrological rectification methodology. Lana was born in Kiev, Ukraine; owing to her place of birth, and like many of her future clients, Lana's birth certificate had no birth time information. For 15 years Lana devoured every book she could find in either English or Russian that offered rectification analysis, and she sought out astrologers in every corner of the world who could share their experience and knowledge with her.

In this webinar, Lara will share her experience of whats worked using Western rectification techniques (and what hasnt) including:

  • Quick and simple technique to validate accuracy of client's birth data
  • Client pre-rectification interview and rectification questionnaire
  • Pre-rectification natal chart analysis
  • Chart rectification using astrological software
  • Final analysis, narrowing down your choices
  • Case histories

Chart rectification isnt beginner astrology, so come with your knowledge of natal chart delineation, transits, solar arc directions and secondary progressions.
