Who We Are

Keuka College is the national leader in experiential, hands-on learning and a national leader in career identification and exploration.

Founded in 1890 on the shores of Keuka Lake in New York's Finger Lakes region, Keuka is a private institution offering affordable degree programs at our Keuka Park campus, more than 30 community colleges and hospitals throughout New York state, four universities in China, and Vietnam National University.
