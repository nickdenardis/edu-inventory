Welcome to Florida Medical Training Institute

Florida Medical Training Institute is dedicated to providing the highest quality education in the allied health occupations, enabling our students to become certified and confident with their knowledge and skills, thereby improving the overall quality of their community.


Attention all National Registered Paramedics & EMT's!

The NREMT is requiring all members complete a recertification program to “Bridge” from old to new curriculums. This is required to be completed by your 2014 recertification cycle.

FMTI will be providing the required “Bridge” curriculums as of January 2012.Do not delay or risk loosing your certification.
