Welcome to KGI
KGI educates future leaders of the bioscience industry through distinctive Professional Science Master's and PhD programs offering integrated life science/engineering/business curriculums and focusing on industry projects and collaborations. Degree programs include our Master of Bioscience (MBS) and Postdoctoral Professional Masters in Bioscience Management (PPM). We also offer the Postbaccalaureate Premedical Certificate Program (PPC) for aspiring medical school students.

KGI Community Mourns the Loss of Bioscience Industry Icon George Rathmann
April 26, 2012

Amgen founder George Rathmann, who is widely credited with being one of the creators of the bioscience industry, died on April 22 at his Palo Alto home. His mix of scientific acumen and entrepreneurship made him a role model for the KGI community.

Tina S. Nova PhD to Speak at KGI Commencement
April 3, 2012
Keck Graduate Institute is pleased to announce that Tina S. Nova, PhD will be the featured speaker at commencement activities on May 12, 2012.  Dr. Nova has served as the President and Co-founder of Genoptix, Inc., a Novartis company, since its inception.
