No Walls. No Ceiling. No Limits.

Welcome to the New Movement in Online Education.

Imagine yourself joining thousands of other students who have enrolled in an online program with options to help you pursue your personal and educational goals through a wide variety of programs. Pursue your online degree from the comfort of your own home or anywhere you choose at a regionally accredited university* that's designed to help fit into your life.

Pursuing online degrees hardly means going it alone. We've built in countless touch points, including our live interactive classes, archived class sessions, 24/7 technical support, student advising, and good old-fashioned instructor office hours. With our Smartphone app, you can have your online training in the palm of your hand.

* Colorado Technical University is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools (230 South LaSalle Street, Suite 7-500, Chicago, Illinois 60604-1411).

Meet Your Professional Network

Earning a degree online at CTU allows you to plug in to a network of students and instructors, extracurricular clubs, webinars and other resources. Our Career Services staff is dedicated to assisting you with resume tips, preparing for interviews, career planning and professional networking. From the moment you begin a degree program until you start on your next career goal, Colorado Technical University can give you tools that can help you become knowledgeable about your chosen field.

Online Education Tailored to You

Colorado Technical University's award-winning** Virtual Campus can allow you to take control of your college education. With our innovative technology, My Unique Student Experience (M.U.S.E.), you can watch, download, read and practice until you're confident in your knowledge of the course material. Each of the tools created for M.U.S.E. is tailored to suit various learning styles and to help you connect what you learn to real-life situations you could encounter in the workplace.

You want an educational approach built to fit the way you function. You want a dynamic, immersive experience that can help you absorb knowledge like you absorb the world around you. You want to collaborate in real time.

CTU's technology can deliver.

Interact with an award-winning** multi-media learning dashboard that lets you watch, download, read, and practice your lessons until you own the knowledge.

Connect to the professional world through faculty, many of whom have experience in the fields they teach.

Network and collaborate across the whole nation, not just a single classroom.
