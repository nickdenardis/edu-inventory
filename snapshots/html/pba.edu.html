  • About PBA

    Enlightening minds ... Enriching Souls ... Extending Hands ...

    Founded in 1968, Palm Beach Atlantic is an interdenominational Christian university with some 50 majors (including nursing), and offering graduate degrees in business, leadership, psychology and pharmacy. Our location in Palm Beaches of Florida makes PBA the ideal place to grow as a student and a person, while exploring your faith with young people of many races, nationalities and creeds.
     

  • Hot Spot

    Record Number Receive Degrees at Spring Commencement

    PBA Interim President William M.B. Fleming Jr. conferred degrees on more than 500 graduates Saturday (5/5) during the Spring Commencement ceremony at the Palm Beach County Convention Center in West Palm Beach. The 540 graduates receiving degrees was the largest number ever from PBA's eight schools.
     

  • Focus On

    Former British Prime Minister Blair Highlights Conference

    Former British Prime Minister Tony Blair (right) sits down for a Q&A with LifeChurch.tv founder/pastor Craig Groeschel during the World LEADERS Conference held April 18-19. Blair spoke to a full house at PBA's DeSantis Family Chapel. Other speakers at the World LEADERS Conference included journalist Cokie Roberts and former NFL/Super Bowl winning coach Tony Dungy.

  • Athletics

    PBA's Sailfish at Home in Top-Notch Greene Complex

    Many of Palm Beach Atlantic's teams play in the outstanding Greene Complex for Sports and Recreation -- a 65,000 square foot facility located in the heart of campus that houses the Rubin Arena, locker rooms, athletic training facility, fitness center, recreational space, PBA Sports Hall of Fame and the PBA Athletic Department.
