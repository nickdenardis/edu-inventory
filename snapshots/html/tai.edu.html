Welcome to Tai Sophia

We are an accredited graduate school, offering master's degrees in Acupuncture, Health and Wellness Coaching, Oriental Medicine, Nutrition and Integrative Health, Therapeutic Herbalism, and Transformative Leadership and Social Change, graduate certificates in Health and Wellness Coaching, Chinese Wellness Therapies, Ayurvedic Wellness Practices,  Herbal Studies, Medical Herbalism, Transformative Leadership, and Chinese Herbs, and post-master's certificates in Animal Acupuncture and Women's Holistic Health.

We are a multi-faceted wellness resource, providing community wellness programs, continuing professional education, and clinical services.

Our beautiful 12-acre campus also houses a bookstore, library, art gallery, and meditation and herb gardens — all of which are open to the public — as well as an herbal dispensary.
