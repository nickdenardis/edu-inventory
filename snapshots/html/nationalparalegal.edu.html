NPC News

Welcome to National Paralegal College!

 

Our nationally accredited programs will enable you to earn your paralegal certificate in just 7 months, your paralegal associate's degree in 15 months, or your bachelor's degree in legal studies in under 3 years.

Receive a 2-week no-cost trial enrollment by completing our school application. Attend live online lectures and experience NPC’s virtual classroom. Interact with our attorney instructors and with your classmates, all from the comfort of your home or office.

Discover for yourself why, in a survey of our students, reviewed by our accrediting agency, nearly all of those surveyed responded that they were “satisfied with their studies at NPC and would recommend the program to a friend.”
