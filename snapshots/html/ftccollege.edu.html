Whatever your unique situation, if you're looking for the skills and training it takes to secure a job with a solid future, Florida Technical College can help.

With many people facing layoffs and uncertain job futures, career prospects and job security are more important than ever. Career opportunities are currently growing in the following areas:

  • Information Technology
  • Healthcare
  • Criminal Justice/Paralegal

In fact, according to the U.S. Bureau of Labor, Healthcare, Networking and Law include some of the fastest growing occupations this decade. If you're looking for career training in one of these exciting and secure occupations, FTC is the place to start.

We're a private junior college with four campuses in Florida. From the moment you set foot on a Florida Technical College campus, you feel the energy. We're a community of individuals working toward career goals including improved income, increased opportunities and job satisfaction.

Our dedicated staff at FTC are committed to assisting you, making a difference in your life, and helping you turn your career dreams into a reality.

To learn more about how Florida Technical College can help you launch a rewarding professional career, browse our site and explore the various career training opportunities open to you.
