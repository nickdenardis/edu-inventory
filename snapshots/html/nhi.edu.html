Why do people choose massage for their new career?

We asked a few of our students and alumni why they're happy they made the choice to go into the massage therapy field.

  • Helping Others is Rewarding

    “Feel the satisfaction of helping others. Massage therapists can help others like few other careers.”

  • Greater Independence and Control of Your Time

    “Be your own boss and control your schedule. Choose full-time or part-time work. You're in control.”

  • Balance and Happiness

    “Better balance between professional and personal lives are reported by many of our graduates.”

  • Great Income Potential

    “Demand for massage is growing quickly, providing multiple income earning opportunities.”

  • Boundless Job Opportunities

    “The U.S. Department of Labor reports that massage therapy jobs will grow twice as fast as other jobs.”

NHI places more than 84% of alumni into massage careers!
