Welcome to Heritage Bible College!

Heritage Bible College of Dunn, North Carolina, is a coeducational Bible college founded in 1971 and sponsored by the Pentecostal Free Will Baptist denomination. Heritage was established to provide systematic and comprehensive training in the Word of God from a Pentecostal perspective to enable students to fulfill their specific call to the ministry. Heritage Bible College welcomes students from all denominations who wish to pursue the College's programs.

Club 1660

Financial Aid News Brief

The financial aid office is currently processing and packaging 2012/2013 federal aid. If you have not completed your FAFSA, please submit as soon as possible to insure that funding is available if you are eligible.

Federal Financial Aid IRS Retrieval Tool Update
The U.S. Department of Education recently created an enhancement to the FAFSA filing process by allowing applicants and parents to retrieve their income and tax data directly from the IRS. This new process is called the IRS Data Retrieval Tool. We strongly recommend that parents and students select this option when filing their 2012-2013 FAFSA. This option will streamline and expedite the processing of your financial aid. The IRS Data Retrieval Tool also lowers the chances of being selected and needing to submit additional documents to our office.
If you are eligible to use the IRS Data Retrieval Tool, we highly recommend using the tool for several reasons:
  1. Its the easiest way to provide your tax data.
  2. Its the best way of ensuring that your FAFSA has accurate tax information.
  3. You wont need to provide a copy of your or your parents tax returns to your college.
