Online Programs at Remington College

At Remington College, the foundation for our online education model is student-centered, and focused on your needs. This accredited online college offers both online associate's degrees and online bachelor degrees which can be completed in as few as 18 months (for our associate's degrees) or 36 months (for our bachelor's degrees).

As a student, you will learn in a virtual environment in the comfort of your own surroundings, but you will not be alone. Our online learning support staff is available to help you from admission through graduation and our courses are designed to create interaction between classmates.

  • Each course lasts five-weeks with breaks between courses.
  • Learn from faculty with real-world experience.
  • Online courses are enhanced through interactive curriculum.
  • Access to the Career Services Team who can help you develop job search skills.
  • Student Services Team provides support to help keep your studies on track.
  • Interaction with instructors in lectures, discussions and Q & A sessions.
