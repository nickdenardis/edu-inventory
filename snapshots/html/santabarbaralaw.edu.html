Credentials

State Bar Accredited

The Colleges of Law have been accredited by the Committee of Bar Examiners of the State Bar of California since the 1970s. Our graduates are eligible to take the California Bar Exam and to practice law in California, upon passing the Bar Exam and meeting the State Bar's other admission requirements.

Juris Doctorate

Flexible Evening Program

Inspired to meet the challenge of a professional career in law?

Our convenient, affordable law degree program makes it possible to earn a J.D. in 3 1/2 to 4 years, attending classes only three nights a week. Tailored to meet the needs of working adults, our curriculum grounds students in the legal theory and practical skills an ethical attorney needs to practice law in 21st century California.
