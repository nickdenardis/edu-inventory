About RCCD

Riverside Community College District is the largest community college system in the Inland Southern California region.

Serving a 540 sq. mile area and seven cities in western Riverside County, the districtâ€™s colleges and education centers educate and train nearly 50,000 students each year. Graduates transfer to four-year colleges and universities throughout the nation or directly enter careers in fields such as law enforcement, firefighting, dental hygiene, nursing, logistics, performing arts, business and computer technology.

RCCD is a major employer in the area and also works with local businesses, providing customized business training, international business and market development, and other services to build and strengthen our communities.
