About Erie Institute of Technology

Erie Institute of Technology or EIT is an Erie Pennsylvania technical or trade school providing accredited training programs for computer, electronics, manufacturing, technology careers.

Training programs include Welding, RHVAC, Electrican, Graphic Design, CNC, Network and Database Administrator, and Biomedical Equipment Technology.
