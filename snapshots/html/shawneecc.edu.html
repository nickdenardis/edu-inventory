SCC Presidential Search Update
The search committee has met on two occasions, March 12th and April 16th.
The committee reviewed applications from an excellent field of candidates. The screening process was concluded with the committee narrowing a group of candidates that will be forwarded to the Board of Trustees on May 7th.
The Chairman of the Board would like to take this opportunity to thank the search committee members for their time and effort.
The Board of Trustees looks forward to coming out of this process with a well qualified president who will move the college forward in the future.

Don E. Patton
Chairman of the Board

42nd Annual SCC Commencement May 11th
The Shawnee Community College Board of Trustees along with the administration, faculty, and staff welcome the graduates and their friends and family to the 42nd Annual Commencement Ceremony on Friday evening, May 11. The program will be in the Edward M. Smith Gymnasium beginning at 7 p.m. Prospective graduates will receive commencement details related to rehearsal information and how to obtain their regalia. SCC Art Club Travels to Memphis
Members of the Shawnee Community College Art Club returned recently from a visit to the Brooks Art Museum in Memphis, TN. As one of the largest and most innovative art museums in the American South, the Brooks Art Museum is recognized nationally for the museum’s innovative educational programs, continual community outreach, and world-class art collection. Students and Art club sponsor Mike Faris also toured historic downtown Memphis.
