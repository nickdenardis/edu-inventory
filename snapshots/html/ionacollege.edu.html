In Memory of

Reverend Dr. John Hoffman
Reverend Dr. Lloyd Smith

and

Gwendolyn Salter

*

John Hoffman was an ordained United Church minister and the first principal of Iona College in 1964.   He served as principal for 21 years. Iona College later named its auditorium after him.

*

Lloyd Smith was an ordained United Church minister.  He served as Iona’s Chancellor from March 2007 to March 2010.

*

Gwen Salter was a teacher, graduate of the University of Windsor, and did her course work at Iona College for her Licensed Lay Worship Leader certificate.   Gwen supported Iona College with her generosity in many ways.


I fear no foe, with Thee at hand to bless;
Ills have no weight, and tears no bitterness;
Where is death’s sting? Where, grave, thy victory?
I triumph still, if Thou abide with me.
