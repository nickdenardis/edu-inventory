The Beginning of Wisdom

What can you find at Southwestern?

You'll find a place where "Christ is Lord" -- in our classrooms and labs, in our dorms and on our athletic fields.

Learn more about our education, which prepares you for life, not just a job.

Spiritual Environment

When we say that we offer a Christ-centered education, we mean that Christianity pervades our whole campus. Our spiritual environment is not something switched on for Friday night or special occasions.

Jesus Christ shapes our assumptions, our questions, our world view.

Learn More

Creating Opportunity

At Southwestern we are dedicated to spotting talent, encouraging "late bloomers," and creating opportunities.

The distinguishing mark of this University is students whose lives have been transformed.
