View Upper Iowa University's World Wide Possibilities.

Upper Iowa offers both undergraduate and graduate degrees, with over 40 academic majors to choose from in liberal arts, business, education, math and science, nursing, and more. UIU ranks among the top 25% of colleges nationally for enhancing student learning (CLA Learning Report 2006-2010).

Once a Peacock, always a Peacock! As an Upper Iowa graduate, the Office of Advancement and Alumni encourages you to stay connected with your University and to participate in the many alumni programs and events they plan especially for you.

UIU is the only NCAA Division II Athletic Program in Iowa and member of Northern Sun Intercollegiate Conference. The UIU Athletics Program strives to serve a diverse population, while sponsoring programs that will foster growth and knowledge for each individual student-athlete.

The largest capital improvement project in UIU's history is underway. Recently completed are a new Liberal Arts Building, Student Center, and suite-style housing facility. Future projects include football complex renovations, science/math building, eCenter building, and additional suite-style housing facilities.

UIU has been named a top Military-Friendly college by Military Advanced Education and GI Jobs magazine and offers tuition discounts for the Total Military Family on campus, at five education centers on U.S. military bases and worldwide through our distance education programs.

Upper Iowa has 17 Center locations across the U.S. that offer evening and weekend classes—the perfect choice if you work full-time, part-time or are looking for a college in your community to realize your academic goals.

UIU is proud to have three international campuses located in the Pacific Rim, that offer undergraduate programs in business, communication, and psychology to learners in Hong Kong, Malaysia and Singapore. UIU also offers a full on-line MBA program targeted at international learners across Asia.

What can Upper Iowa University offer you? Everything. Whether you're looking for an on-campus community, educational centers near your home, or global access to online and independent study courses, we've got it. Pick from more than 40 majors, master's degrees, bachelor's degrees, associate degrees and undergraduate certificates in a variety of specialties. Fully accredited with more than 150 years of experience providing quality education, Upper Iowa is the University in your future.
