Chiropractic School - Life Chiropractic College West

Thank you very much for your interest in the chiropractic profession, a chiropractic school, and Life Chiropractic College West. Choosing an excellent chiropractic college is your first step toward entering a rewarding and fast-growing profession. As a chiropractic college, our institution is dedicated to producing caring, capable, and chiropractic school graduates who thrive in their practices, and in their respective communities as Doctors of Chiropractic. Our chiropractic school focuses on providing an integrated educational system that places emphasis on the patient's overall well-being. By evaluating factors including diet, exercise, rest, environment and heredity, Life Chiropractic College West teaches its chiropractic school students to use natural, drug-free, non-surgical, health treatments that rely on the body's self-healing mechanisms.

What sets Life Chiropractic College West apart from other chiropractic colleges and chiropractic schools is our dynamic environment. Here you can achieve your dream of becoming a Doctor of Chiropractic that truly makes a difference to the holistic health of your community. We provide exceptional chiropractic school training and exposure to a greater variety of chiropractic techniques than any of the other chiropractic colleges and chiropractic schools. We offer cutting edge technology, personalized mentorship from our diverse and experienced faculty, and phenomenal support from our chiropractic schools staff so you are well equipped to launch a successful career as a Doctor of Chiropractic.

Life West - The Chiropractic College For You!

As a chiropractic school student, and future doctor of chiropractic, you influence the lives of others because your efforts focus on maximizing your patient's potential for optimum physical, mental, social and spiritual well-being. As a chiropractic school, we encourage our students to internalise the qualities of dedication and commitment that we feel are essential to your education and chiropractic colleges training as a Doctor of Chiropractic. As a gatekeeper for direct access to the health delivery system, the responsibilities of a chiropractic school grad and Doctor of Chiropractic include wellness promotion, health assessments, diagnosis, and the chiropractic management of the patient's health care needs. Life Chiropractic College West will seek to develop your minds while in chiropractic school – as well as your hands and your heart. In so doing, we believe our chiropractic school can prepare you to excel as a caregiver, and to become a trusted, valuable resource for your patients and your community.

Life West Highest in Alumni Satisfaction of Chiropractic Colleges & Chiropractic Schools

For the third year in a row, Life Chiropractic College West alumni continue to honor their alma mater in an independent survey administered and tabulated annually by New York Chiropractic College (NYCC) regarding chiropractic colleges and chiropractic schools. Our analysis of the data demonstrates how Life Chiropractic College West was ranked number one out of all the chiropractic colleges and chiropractic schools! It can be found at Alumni Poll 2009 Results. In short, Life Chiropractic College West accumulated the greatest number of top level marks after reviewing all of the responses for all of the chiropractic colleges and chiropractic schools.

In a recent independent nationwide survey among 12 chiropractic colleges and chiropractic schools, Life Chiropractic College West alumni ranked highest of all the participating chiropractic colleges and chiropractic schools in the key areas of "satisfaction being a chiropractor" and with the "overall value of your education." Among the respondents from all participating chiropractic colleges and chiropractic schools, the Life Chiropractic College West grads also expressed among the highest levels of "feelings of success," of being "prepared to effectively use techniques and treatments," and of being "prepared to manage patient care" after chiropractic school.
