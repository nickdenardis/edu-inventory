Darton College To Host Professional Women's Forum On May 16

Darton College’s Continuing Education will host a Professional Women’s Forum on Wednesday, May 16, from 11:30 a.m. – 1 p.m. at Doublegate Country Club. The featured speaker is Caroline Fielding, Dean of Darton’s Cordele Center. She will speak on “From Tear Gas to Sweet Tea, One Woman’s Journey”.

Fielding, who has worked in post-secondary education for more than 20 years, was named director of the Cordele Center in 2006. Since then she has played an instrumental role in the development of the center, increasing enrollment each year and expanding the educational opportunities available to the area. Fielding also is very active in the community. She has served on many boards, including the Rotary Club of Dougherty County, the United Way of Southwest Georgia’s Executive Committee, and the Albany Area Chamber of Commerce Legislative Affairs Committee. Currently, Fielding serves on the Cordele-Crisp Chamber of Commerce board and the Cordele Rotary Club board.
