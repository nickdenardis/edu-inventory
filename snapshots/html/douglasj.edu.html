Attend School at a Douglas J Aveda Institute

Beauty School, Done Better

At a Douglas J Aveda Institute, students receive a world-class education in cosmetology and esthiology, preparing them for creative, rewarding and successful careers in the salon and spa industries.

Our beauty schools promote the unique Aveda philosophy of learning that education is about more than learning technical skills - it is about growing as an individual. Douglas J Aveda Institutes help students to not only master their craft -- whether hair cutting, hair coloring, nail or spa and skin care -- but also to be their best as an artist and professional.

Our Mission

The Douglas J Aveda Institute's mission is to provide "Education Beyond Expectation" by preparing its graduates for careers in cosmetology arts and sciences and/or related fields and/or unrelated fields.

The Douglas J Aveda Institute's mission to provide "Education Beyond Expectation" is achieved by:

  • Providing a state-of-the-art educational facility and cutting edge training;
  • Fostering an atmosphere of respect and trust to ensure students are free to perfect their craft and express their individualism and creativity;
  • Instilling a sense of professionalism, teamwork, and collaboration with colleagues;
  • Training students to provide comprehensive, outstanding client service.

Why You Should Join Us

  1. We have a 98% state board pass rate. While receiving your cutting edge education, your educators will also help you prepare for your State Board Licensing examination. You could be licensed in as little as one month after you graduate.
  2. Most of our graduates stay in the Aveda Network and Aveda salons charge more. And when salons charge more, stylists make more.Aveda stylists salaries are 25% higher than the industry average. Many salons offer insurance benefits, educational opportunities and career development.
  3. People love Aveda. Dubbed a Love Brand in the book Trading Up: The New American Luxury (Michael Silverstein, Neill Fiske), Aveda has major branding power, and so do Douglas J Aveda Institute graduates. Because Aveda is so well recognized as an exclusive brand, customers seek it out when looking for a new salon.
  4. We raise the bar. Youll be treated as a professional from day one, and our high standards lead to superior results. We dont just teach techniquewe teach the thought process.
