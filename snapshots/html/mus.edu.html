Montana University System

The Montana University System (MUS) is comprised of fourteen public universities and colleges, enrolling more than 47,000 students each semester. This website is designed to provide students, parents, and the public easy access to critical information about enrolling and succeeding in the Montana University System, such as, information on preparing for college, financial aid, transfer of credits, and distance learning.
