About Oklahoma Health Academy

Oklahoma Health Academy originally began vocational training in August of 1997. Oklahoma Health Academy has continued to expand its presence in the field of Allied Health, by adding to its existing Medical Assistant/Phlebotomy program a Dental Assistant program and a Massage Therapy program.

OHA Programs Offered

Oklahoma Health Academy is accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges, and is licensed by the Oklahoma Board of Private Vocational Schools. All of the programs offered by the school meet the requirements of Federal and/or State laws regarding Student Financial Assistance.

  • Medical Assistant / Phlebotomy 
  • Dental Assistant 
  • Massage Therapy

As a Graduate from Oklahoma Health Academy, you will be ready to begin that new career immediately with the knowledge and experience that only hands-on training can provide.

Oklahoma Health Academy believes that placement assistance is an ongoing process, which begins well in advance of graduation. This is accomplished by providing the student with medical/technical training within the health community, teaching the student how to search out job openings, write a resume and handle job interviews. This process serves to prepare the student for a challenging career in the health care industry.
