The Healthcare Industry

Healthcare is a rapidly evolving industry that changes frequently as new research, trends and advancements are discovered.

According to the recent U.S. Department of Labor Bureau of Labor Statistics, healthcare workers at all levels of education and training will continue to be in demand. In many cases, it may be easier for jobseekers with health-specific training to obtain jobs and advance in their careers. Specialized clinical training is a requirement for many jobs in healthcare and is an asset even for many administrative jobs that do not specifically require it.* Click here to learn more.

*Source: Bureau of Labor Statistics, U.S. Department of Labor, Career Guide to Industries, 2008-09 Edition, Health Care, on the Internet at http://www.bls.gov/oco/cg/pdf/cgs035.pdf (visited July 19, 2009). This is a national estimate and conditions in your location may be different.
