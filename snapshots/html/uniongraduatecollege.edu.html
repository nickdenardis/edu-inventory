The emphasis of Union Graduate College is on highly effective education that provides the skills and knowledge that today's employers want. As a result, our students graduate ready to make an impact in their chosen professions immediately and over the long term.
Union Graduate College is a graduate-only institution, dedicated to providing exceptional MBA programs, including an MBA in Healthcare Management, secondary education, engineering, and clinical leadership and bioethics. Our singular focus is reflected in the reputation of our faculty, the strength of our programs, and the caliber of students that we attract. 		  News
