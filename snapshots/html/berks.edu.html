Welcome

Berks Technical Institute’s Focused Learning Courses

Our Degree Training Programs Prepare You For an Exciting Career!

At Berks Technical Institute, you'll find a focused learning environment based on you, the student. We believe in a quality education, one that matches your career goals with the needs of today's job market.

Over the years, BTI has expanded its program curriculum to include specialized career education courses in many of the fastest growing fields in the country. We strive to continually provide quality training programs, implemented by a highly competent and dedicated school faculty - giving our students the degree training that is necessary to build success in their chosen careers.
