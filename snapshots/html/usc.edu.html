Book Drive 2012 in Overdrive

The number of visitors to the University Park campus the weekend of April 21-22 — 151,000 people according to the Los Angeles Times — wasn’t the only thing about the 2012 L.A. Times Festival of Books that broke records. USC’s annual book drive brought in a staggering 8,000 books, more than double last year’s donations.

USC Professor Sings Praises of L.A. Music History

Joining forces with the Grammy Museum, USC Annenberg School for Communication and Journalism professor Josh Kun has curated Trouble in Paradise: Music and Los Angeles 1945-1975, the first large-scale exhibition on Los Angeles music by a local museum.

Prof. Tom Lyon’s Research on Child Abuse

USC’s Tom Lyon has received two NIH grants to investigate how to improve the court system’s approach to getting children to talk honestly about abuse. In this video, he discusses how to encourage children to talk about “bad things” they have experienced without tainting their reports.
