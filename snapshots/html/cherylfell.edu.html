Cheryl Fell’s School of Business believes in the overall educational development of the individual so that he/she will be trained to use his/her skills and knowledge and will also develop good judgment enabling him/her to make sound decisions and advance to higher levels as he/she gains in experience and skill.  By keeping its curriculum flexible, the School can make the necessary changes and adjustments to train the student beyond his/her present skills and knowledge.

The primary purpose of the School's existence is to prepare young men and women for entry-level employment in the business occupations.
