Sunday, May 06, 2012
 

Championship Week at SHU

Siena Heights University is hosting both the baseball tournament and the outdoor track and field championships on campus this week for the Wolverine-Hoosier Athletic Conference. For more information, visit www.shusaints.com.

 

New Student Registration Days

Accepted to Siena Heights? Congratulations! Your next step is to sign up for a New Student Registration Day (NSRD). All new students are required to attend a NSRD. Spots are limited, so sign up today!

 

Commencement 2012

We look forward to celebrating Commencement 2012. Commencement is at 3 p.m. Saturday, May 5 in the Fieldhouse on the SHU main campus in Adrian. This ceremony will recognize all degree candidates from all Siena Heights locations.

Alumni & Friends

Stay connected with Siena Heights! Whether you are a graduate or former student, a volunteer, supporter, neighbor or friend, we invite you to engage with what's happening at SHU!

 

New at the SHU...

Siena Heights University announced the addition of a competitive cheer and dance team to its athletic department. The new squads will start in the 2012-13 academic year. The Saints named Angie Sword as their first cheer and dance coach.
