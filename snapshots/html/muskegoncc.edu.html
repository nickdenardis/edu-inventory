Top News: MCC Nursing Program Earns National Accreditation Through 2019

The Muskegon Community College Nursing Program has been granted full continuing accreditation through 2019 by the National League for Nursing Accrediting Commission, Inc. (NLNAC). "The NLNAC is the only national accrediting body for Associate Degree Nursing programs," said Pam Brown, director of MCC's Nursing Program. "Muskegon Community College Nursing Program is among only 13 of the 28 nationally accredited Associate Degree Nursing Programs in Michigan."
