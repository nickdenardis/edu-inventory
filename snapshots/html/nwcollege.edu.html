Welcome to Northwest College, where exciting careers in the spa and salon industries begin!

The spa & salon industries are large and are continually growing, providing abundant opportunities for career advancement and security. Spas & salons continue to open across the country and around the world, creating high demand for licensed hairdressers, manicurists, and skin care specialists. There has never been a better time to start your career in the spa & salon industries.

Northwest College has been providing highest-quality education and services since 1976. Contact us today to see how Northwest College can help you achieve your dreams.
