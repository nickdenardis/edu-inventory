Welcome to Frederick Taylor University

Since its founding in 1994 as a premier institution of higher learning, Frederick Taylor University has provided undergraduate and graduate degrees as well as advanced certificates courses in business administration and management.
With FTU's 100% online learning format, you can now receive the training you need and earn your prestigious Bachelor's and Master's degrees. You have the option to complete your studies in various elective programs such as Marketing, Operations, Information Systems, Human Resources, International Business, Healthcare Administration, Supply Chain Management, Executive Management and other lucrative fields.

Designed by leading scholars from respected colleges and universities, our degree programs utilize distance learning and online formats. As a distance learning student, you can benefit from our customized one-on-one advising with faculty members.

We encourage you to browse through our web site to learn how you can benefit from our self-paced, high quality, and affordable degree programs.
