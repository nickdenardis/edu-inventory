PROGRAM SPOTLIGHT

Social Work

Social work is a profession for those with a strong desire to help improve people’s lives.


Cecil’s Social Work degree option is offered in an accelerated format. A partnership agreement with Salisbury University enables Cecil completers to earn their bachelor’s degree in Social Work right here in Cecil County at Elkton Station.


Career Opportunities:

  • public welfare and community agencies
  • criminal justice/corrections
  • school social work
  • child welfare
  • gerontology and health care
  • research
  • education

For more information, contact Cecil’s Admissions Office.
