Rev. John M. Dreyer has been named Director of Admission for Concordia Theological Seminary, Fort Wayne, Indiana. Rev. Dreyer is a 1992 graduate of CTS. Prior to his service at CTS, Rev. Dreyer served congregations in Iowa, Minnesota and Indiana. Read Full Story...

CTS Offers Workshops for Organists and Pianists in June 2012

Music is an integral part of worship and Concordia Theological Seminary, Fort Wayne, is offering workshops for church organists and pianists to support them in their service. "Recognizing that many congregations' singing is led from pianos and keyboards instead of organs," said Kantor Kevin Hildebrand, "these workshops will provide valuable instruction in how to lead congregational singing with the unique features of those instruments." The workshops are for organists/pianists who are already playing, whether they are experienced veterans or new organists/pianists recently drafted into helping at their parish. Read Full Story...
