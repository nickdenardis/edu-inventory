Grace College of Divinity

Welcome to Grace College of Divinity! Grace College of Divinity is a biblical higher education institution dedicated to Preparing Emerging Leaders to Change the World by advancing the Kingdom of God through sound biblical training, practical ministry and personal development.

Students at Grace College of Divinity (GCD) are passionate about life and ministry. We acknowledge our students as unique individuals, guiding them in the discovery of their particular strengths. We continually engage in tailoring their development and transformation so they may become integrated leaders of action.

GCD students feel confident, empowered, and prepared to influence their world.

We are located in Fayetteville, North Carolina on the main campus of Manna Church, one of the top 100 fastest growing churches in the United States (Outreach Magazine, 2011).

The Theological Journal

We are currently receiving article entries for Volume II of our Theological Journal. This is open to students and faculty.

Steps to Getting Your Articles Published

  1. Choose a topic and submit your idea to gcd@gcdivinity.org.
  2. Once your topic has been approved, you may begin work on your article (5-15 pages in length, Chicago Style format).
  3. Submit your article to gcd@gcdivinity.org for faculty approval.
