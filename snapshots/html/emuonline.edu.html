Need additional help signing in?

Students: Your student ID number (EID) serves as your login for EMU-Online.

If you are new to the EMU–Online system, (which is different than the my.emich system), you should retrieve your randomly generated password, log in and change your password. Your password will be sent to your emich email account.
If you have done this in a prior term, please log in using the credentials you established at that time.

Please click "Forgot your Password?" above to begin the password retrieval process.

For assistance, please contact our 24/7 help desk at 888.538.0515.
