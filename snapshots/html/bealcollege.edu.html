Beal College - Your Career Awaits!

With new courses starting July 2nd, there's never been a better time to explore Beal!

Success in college is all about finding the right fit. At Beal College, we believe in doing things differently.

Why?  Because Beal College is not your typical college – and our students are not your typical students!

• We are a small, private, locally owned college with 120 years of service to Maine citizens. 
• We partner with our students to support them through their academic journeys. 
• We offer a more convenient class schedule with more opportunities to begin your education.
• Our students come from all ages and backgrounds.
• We provide affordable, practical education that fosters hands-on learning.
• Financial Aid is available to those who qualify.
• We prepare our students for life after Beal College.
• Our graduates see the results of their Beal College education.

At Beal College, we believe in our students and we believe in their success.

The Right Career for You!

Our admissions staff understands the importance of selecting the right career path.  They’re trained to help you identify an opportunity and pursue an education to achieve it.  Work with one of our admission specialists today to start your new career.

Beal's Mission:

To become partners with our students in their journey to improve their career opportunities through the contemporary academic programs offered at Beal College.

Beal's Vision:

Beal College will become the region's premier career college by focusing on academic programs and associated student support services that are both qualitatively and quantitatively effective.
