Business Administration

The Associate of Science Degree in Business Administration at Daytona College provides students an introduction to the fundamentals of business administration in preparation for entry level careers in business administration.

Allied Health

Are you interested in a career in healthcare? Want to work hands-on with patients and physicians in a number of different environments? Daytona College provides a solid foundation which prepares graduates for an entry-level career in medical assisting, medical billing and coding and health information technology.

Aesthetics Skin Care

In a nation obsessed with the way they look, the skincare industry continues to grow. The Aesthetics Skin Care Program at Daytona College prepares graduates who are qualified to pursue careers in Day Spas, Medical Skin Care Clinics, Resorts and also Dermatology offices.

Therapeutic Massage

Therapeutic Massage is widely thought of as a way to reduce stress and provide holistic relief for a range of soft-tissue pain and joint dysfunction. Our program prepares upcoming graduates for testing and licensure by Florida Board of Massage.  They are then ready to enter a respected career as Licensed Massage Therapists.

Cosmetology

Prepare for an exciting career in cosmetology with comprehensive Pivot Point training from Daytona College. Completing the program prepares you to sit for your Florida Board of Cosmetology licensure examination.  Once licensed as a Cosmetologist, you are prepared for many opportunities in the field.

Welcome to Daytona College

Daytona College is a private college located in the beautiful and historic city of Ormond Beach, Florida, just north of Daytona Beach.  We offer a range of Diploma and Associate of Science Degree programs designed to prepare students to enter new careers in their chosen fields.

Career areas of study include Allied Health, Beauty and Wellness, and Business programs. While some of our programs may be completed totally online, we also offer a blended approach of classroom instruction, online study and on-campus lab and clinic practice in some majors.  Residential programs offer a mix of theory and hands on training to prepare for the work place.

Whether you’re just starting to think about a career, or looking to change your current work situation, Daytona College offers a focused career education to get you trained and ready to start a new career.

What the Starfish Means to Us

According to legend, starfish are reflections of stars that inhabit the ocean floor. Symbolizing rebirth, starfish have a remarkable ability for regeneration. Each limb works as part of the whole, and if just one limb breaks off, a new one grows in its place.

The Starfish is often associated with characteristics such as guidance, vigilance, inspiration, brilliance and intuition. In a sense, it is a symbol of life moving forward.

As each new student walks through our doors with their own unique goals and aspirations, we feel it is our responsibility to help you learn to nurture that goal and build the foundation needed toward a new career, and more important, a new start on life.

At Daytona College, we feel much like the Starfish, many limbs working towards keeping each symbol of life moving forward.  Our administration truly believes that every resource necessary should be put forth to ensure that each individual student that passes through our doors not only thrives, but shines.
