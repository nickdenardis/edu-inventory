Closure of Bethany University of the Assemblies of God

On August 8, 2011 a Teach-out Plan to facilitate the closure of Bethany University was submitted to the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC) and has subsequently received approval on August 16, 2011.

Teach-out Plan

Vanguard University of Southern California has also submitted and received approval from WASC for a Memorandum of Understanding for the purpose of being the eventual repository of Bethany University’s academic student records.
