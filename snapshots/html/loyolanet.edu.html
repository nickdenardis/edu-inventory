Photos: Centennial Celebration

See more photos from our centennial kick off celebration »

 

Loyola held the kick off to its year-long centennial celebration, April 13-15, 2012. Events included alumni reunion dinners, a centennial exhibition in the J. Edgar and Louise S. Monroe Library, lectures about Loyola's history, concert and chorale performances, a centennial Mass, and an anniversary picnic in the Marquette Horseshoe.
