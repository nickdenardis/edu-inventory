Welcome to
Thomas Jefferson University

Thomas Jefferson University and Thomas Jefferson University Hospitals are partners in providing excellent clinical and compassionate care for our patients in the Philadelphia region, educating the health professionals of tomorrow in a variety of disciplines and discovering new knowledge that will define the future of clinical care.

Thomas Jefferson University is dedicated to health sciences education and research.

Jefferson College of Graduate Studies
Biomedical Science PhD and MS (including Professional Science Masters) degree programs; Graduate Certificate programs; Postdoctoral Affairs.

Jefferson
Medical College
Founded in 1824, JMC offers undergrad medical education programs and innovative joint degree programs to more than 1,000 students each year.
Jefferson School of Health Professions
Bioscience Technologies, Couple and Family Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Physical Therapy, Radiologic Sciences and General Studies.



Jefferson
School of Nursing
A continuum of nursing programs, from bachelor through doctorate degrees, including a variety of programs for RNs.

Jefferson
School of Pharmacy
The 4-year Doctor of Pharmacy (PharmD) degree for students with at least 2 years of college credits.


Jefferson School of Population Health
JSPH provides graduate academic programming, continuing education and research in health policy, public health and healthcare quality and safety.
