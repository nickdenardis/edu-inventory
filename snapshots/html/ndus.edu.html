The North Dakota University System includes 11 campuses: two world-class research institutions, four outstanding regional universities and five vibrant community colleges.

Each campus offers different learning and living experiences, and all share a commitment to excellence and personal and professional growth. Whether students are preparing to provide health care, teach math, manage businesses, conduct research or overhaul trucks, when they leave our campuses they are prepared to be the best in their fields.

Our investment in technology and the connectedness of our campuses give University System students incredible access to instruction, no matter where they live. Through partnerships with business, industry and government agencies, the campuses provide training, conduct research and stimulate economic growth - brightening the future for all of us.

Access. Excellence. Innovation. All are hallmarks of the North Dakota University System.
