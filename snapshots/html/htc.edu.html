Lekutei Peshatim

Lekutei Peshatim is a publication of weekly Divrei Torah and community happenings distributed in synagogues throughout Chicago and suburbs. Lekutei Peshatim are prepared by the faculty, Kollel, and student body of Beis HaMidrash LaTorah.
