College Classes in High School!

Beginning Spring 2012, the North Carolina Career and College Promise will replace Huskins, Concurrent Enrollment, and Learn and Earn Online dual enrollment programs. This program offers three pathways designed to give motivated high school students a head start on your college and career training!

Spring Semester Mini Sessions

It's not too late to sign up for a class THIS SEMESTER at CFCC. Two spring mini-sessions are starting next month! Earn full credit in half the time. Classes range from accounting, fiberglass boat repair, psychology, Spanish, algebra and much more!
