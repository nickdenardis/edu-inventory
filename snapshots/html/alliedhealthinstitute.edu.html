Allied Health Institute

Online Health and Medical Assistant School

Pursue Your Future in Healthcare

Allied Health Institute is a post-secondary school that offers a variety of degree and diploma programs in the allied health field through distance education.  By utilizing the internet, students have the flexibility to earn a diploma that allows them to enter the workforce or change careers from the convenience of their own home.  Our virtual classrooms will enable students to attend classes and communicate with instructors and classmates.  Our motto explains our objective: "Closing the Distance in Allied Health".  Our concept is based on providing the opportunity to gain education in the medical field, regardless of the studentís day-to-day obligations.

At Allied Health Institute (AHI), we are dedicated to helping men and women develop the skills and knowledge to pursue many opportunities in the field of allied health.  We offer online education opportunities for students to pursue their educational objectives when attending school in a traditional classroom is not practical.  At AHI, we understand the importance of your future.  If you would like to find out if an Allied Health Institute education is right for you, give us a call.  If you have any specific questions about our Online Allied Health and Medical Assistant Programs, call us at (954) 491-7777 or click here to contact us.
