Al Jamea Tus Saifiyah is a unique seat of learning different from other Muslim universities for those wishing to have advanced contemporary Islamic education   It offers a holistic education, based on pedagogically-sound, Islamic principles and a unique Fatemi educational methodology. This pedagogy emphasizes a systematic  attempt to re-assess and re-value traditional branches of
study and to blend modern knowledge and experience in Islamic fabric so as to modernize entire outlook to meet new needs and lines of approach. With this result Aljamea’s graduates are effective and empathetic leaders, problem solvers, promoters of human progress and valuable members of society.
Aljamea is the apex institute of the Dawoodi Bohra community whose spiritual leader is His Holiness Syedna Mohammad Burhanuddin.; the 52nd incumbent of the vice Gerency of the Fatimi Imam. 
Through the goodness of his character, a man attains the status of one dedicated to rozah and namaaz.
