Team GCC rides across America
     On May 26, four soon-to-be alumni will begin a bike trip from Seattle to New York City to promote Grove City College, raise money for scholarship and tell the untold stories of alumni and friends. Riding as “Team GCC”, Mackenzie Lloyd '12, Ryan Herman '13, Mark Loosararian '13 and Will Moyer '12 hope that their epic adventure will make a lasting impact. Aligning with the Grove City Matters capital campaign, the College will serve as Team GCC’s lead sponsor as they make their 4,000 mile trek.

Walter E. Williams to headline 2012 Commencement
     Grove City College Trustee, Guest Lecturer in Economics and distinguished author Dr. Walter E. Williams will deliver the keynote address at the 2012 Grove City College Commencement ceremony on Saturday, May 19, at 10 a.m., on the Quad between Harbison Chapel and Crawford Hall of the Grove City College campus. In the case of inclement weather, Commencement will be moved to the Arena of the Physical Learning Center.

Grove City College shines at Tepper Venture Challenge
     The Center for Entrepreneurship & Innovation at Grove City College is proud to announce that two Grove City College teams earned first and second place overall at the prestigious Tepper Venture Challenge last weekend. The annual business plan competition held at Carnegie Mellon University was judged by a board of venture capitalists and featured many other top academic institutions from the area.
