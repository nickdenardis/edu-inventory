Who We Are

  • High Quality Academics

    The Mount delivers high-quality academics, hands-on experiential learning and personalized attention from faculty and staff dedicated to your success. To learn more, visit our academics landing page.

  • Experiential Learning

    We will challenge you to build a career through involvement in cooperative education, service learning, internships and more. To find out how the Mount can prepare you for a successful career, visit the Career and Experiential Learning Center.

  • Personalized Attention

    We offer dedicated faculty who know you: your immediate path and your future goals. Our 12:1 student-to-faculty ratio gives you the chance to work one-on-one with faculty to develop your strengths. Learn more about the Mount.

  • Devotion to Service

    Since our founding in 1920 by the Sisters of Charity, we have made serving the needs of our students and the community our core mission. As a nationally recognized institution, we live a culture of ethics and service in everything we do. Read about our mission.
