CREO 2012 - Just one week away
CREO 2012 is a collaboration among Mount Mary College's Fashion Design, Interior Design, Graphic Design and Fine Arts Programs. The show takes place off-campus at the Downtown Hyatt Hotel in Milwaukee on May 11, from noon to 10:00 p.m. It's a full day of free student gallery shows, a silent auction, elbow-rubbing with Milwaukee's art community and the 45th annual student designer fashion show. In its inaugural year, CREO will feature work inspired by the concept of "Evoke." Jordan Dechambre, Fashion Editor at M Magazine, will emcee the event. Find out more.

  • Save on downtown parking by riding the FREE shuttle from Mount Mary.
