Distance Learning

Earn an M.A. (Master of Arts) in Theology or Philosophy online.  Our non-residential Distance Learning Program allows professionals, service members and people with familial obligations to study at home on a flexible schedule.  Grounded in the tradition of Aristotle and St. Thomas Aquinas, our program introduces the student to major philosophical or theological disciplines of the Catholic Tradition. The Seminary

In its preparation of men for the priesthood, Holy Apostles Seminary is committed to providing solid formation in all aspects of seminary life, in accord with the directives of the Catholic Church. It is guided by the norms established by the Holy See, notably in the Apostolic Exhortation of Pope John Paul II, Pastores Dabo Vobis (1992), and by the norms established by the American Bishops in the Program of Priestly Formation. The College

Offering an A.A. (Associate of Arts); B.A. (Bachelor of Arts); M.A. (Master of Arts); and Post-Masters Certificate, Holy Apostles College welcomes and serves undergraduate and graduate lay commuter students as well as seminarians.  Holy Apostles is a fully accredited, co-educational Catholic college located in Cromwell, Connecticut. Read More
