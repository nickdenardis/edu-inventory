Welcome to McCann.edu

Whether preparing to enter the professional world or expanding your skills, education should be available wherever, whenever. With McCann you can rely on challenging programs, interesting curriculum, and a friendly educational environment.

If quality is what you want then McCann is where you want to be.

No two students are the same and no two learning experiences should be either. With the McCann family you can attend classes at a campus not far from you. From the moment you enroll, you'll see that our focus is you.

From your classes to your education to your employment, you are becoming part of a team that will be there each step of the way. At McCann you can count on innovative technology, experienced instructors, engaging classes, and pioneering techniques.

Enroll today and become part of an institution that has grown since 1897 realizing campuses in Pennsylvania; Hazleton, Dickson City, Sunbury, Pottsville, Allentown, and Carlisle. Each McCann School of Business & Technology is accredited by the Accrediting Council for Independent Colleges and Schools.
