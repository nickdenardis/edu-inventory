Add 100 years of excellence to your resume.

Click to watch the NECB commercial!

New England College of Business and Finance (NECB) has a century-long heritage of education excellence for you to draw from for your next big career step. Founded in 1909 in Boston, Massachusetts, NECB quickly began educating leaders in the business and banking communities. Throughout the years NECB evolved from a regional training institution to a fully accredited college offering online degree programs at the graduate and undergraduate level.

The adoption of an online delivery model has allowed NECB to offer degree programs that are among the most affordable options in higher education. Instructors at NECB come from a blend of seasoned business practitioners and academic researchers. This faculty ensures that NECB students receive instruction in both theoretical and practical approaches to the knowledge they will need to succeed in the workplace. Whether at the undergraduate or graduate level, students often find courses at NECB more "real world" than at some other institutions. NECB graduates invariably look back on their time at the college as having been pivotal in their career development.
