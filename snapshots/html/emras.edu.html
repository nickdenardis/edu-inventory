   Welcome to the El Monte-Rosemead Adult School Web site.

   The adult school offers a variety of classes to meet your academic and occupational/career goals. Our goal is to offer quality service and educational opportunities to the citizens of our communities.

   The adult education faculty and staff are always available to assist you in developing your educational plans. Please visit our online resources for detailed information about our programs.
 

MISSION STATEMENT

The El Monte-Rosemead Adult School will be responsive to the diverse student population and the community that it serves.

Classes will be offered to enhance the knowledge and skills necessary to participate and communicate effectively as productive citizens, workers, parents and family members in a changing society. It is our goal to help students improve their quality of life by becoming problem solvers and lifelong learners.
