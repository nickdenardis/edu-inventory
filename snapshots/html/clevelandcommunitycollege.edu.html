Did You Know?

CCC's web address will be changing on May 25 to clevelandcc.edu

Your student email, Skydrive, and any accounts you have associated with your College email account will be affected. Keep checking your current student email and sign up for cccAlert for more updates.


Access to MyCleveland and student email may be sporadic or unavailable during May 17-25.

Course evaluations are now available
and will remain open until May 12, 2012 at 11:00 PM.

Evaluations are accessible through CCC student email accounts. Students who do not have CCC student email accounts should complete the student email set up process, located under the CCC Connect tab at www.clevelandcommunitycollege.edu. Students who experience difficulty setting up their student email accounts should go to the Help Desk in the Library for assistance.

Results from evaluations will be provided to instructors after grades have been submitted beginning May 14, 2012. Responses are reported in summary form only, to ensure identities of individual participants remain confidential.

CCC Announces cccAlert

CCC is pleased to announce a new service available free to all students. cccAlert is the College’s new instant notification system. With cccAlert, you will receive notice in the event of an emergency or inclement weather and reminders on important dates critical to the operation of Cleveland Community College. You are automatically enrolled with cccAlert through your official student email address. You can sign up for free to receive updates on other personal email accounts as well as your cell phone. You can even connect through Facebook!

Click on the following link to sign up today:
http://www.clevelandcommunitycollege.edu/current_students.php?cat=1397
