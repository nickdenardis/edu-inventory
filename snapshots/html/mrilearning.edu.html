Management Resources Institute (MRI) is a management consulting and human development firm who helps organizations improve their customer satisfaction and profitability through a customized approach. MRI specializes in integrating customer needs with business strategies to achieve total customer loyalty and corporate excellence. Management Resources helps individuals and organizations achieve their full potential.
MRI-Learning is the use of technology to enable people to learn anytime and anywhere. MRI-Learning can include training, the delivery of just-in-time information and guidance from experts MRI-Learning provides the student or learner with information that can be accessed in a setting free from time and place constraints. The student can go through the lessons at his or her own pace
