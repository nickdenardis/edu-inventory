The Hawaii College of Oriental Medicine  (formerly named the Traditional Chinese Medical College Of Hawaii or for short, TCMCH) is an accredited institution dedicated to providing both state of the art training for our students and first class clinical care for our patients.

 We combine a modern integration of Oriental and Western medical courses with thorough clinical training. Our students learn not only to relieve symptoms, but help to bring patients into a mind/body balance that aids in the restoration of health.

 HICOM students become healers in the truest sense of the word.


Join The Fastest Growing Field Of Medicine

We share the excitement that is shared upon learning your life's purpose. That is why we designed our Acupuncturist & Oriental Medicine Program to empower all of our students with the training needed to be successful. It is everything you imagined and more. Once you complete the training you will be prepared to become a licensed Acupuncturist!

Our FAST TRACK enrollment program will help you to get started learning as soon as possible. We are available to answer all your questions and get you started now.

 We are now accepting enrollments for Fall 2012!


 Big Island Of Hawaii = Truly Amazing

Everyone dreams of visiting Hawaii for a wonderful vacation. You can live that dream every day. The islands have so much to offer that includes local Hawaiian culture such as hula, chanting and luau's.  The aina (land) of Hawaii holds a healing energy that is perfect to support you to realize your dreams. We offer you the chance to transform your mind, body and career through our life-changing Acupuncture & Oriental Medicine (Masters of Oriental Medicine - MSOM) Program.

We are passionate about doing our part to end the global health crisis and we need people like you!

Please join us today and become part of the solution.
