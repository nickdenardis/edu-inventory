Automotive Technology

If you like working on cars, you'll love Automotive Technology. You'll receive training in all eight areas of the ASE standards.

Medical/Clinical Assistant

Cross-train for multiple medical positions. Work in a clinic, doctor's office or hospital.

Massage Therapy

Our culture is embracing the benefits of massage therapy. Learn Swedish massage, hydrotherapy, advanced massage techniques and spa treatments.

Refrigeration and HVAC Technology

HVAC technicians diagnose, repair and perform preventative maintenance on a variety of highly sophisticated equipment.

Electronic Engineering Technology

Learn to work on computer workstations, microwave equipment, satellite communications equipment, fiber optics equipment and other electronics.

Information Systems and Security

Learn to integrate computer hardware and software in support of modern networking solutions through Microsoft, Cisco and Linux applications.

Physical Therapist Assistant

Prepare for a career in many different healthcare settings: Acute care, outpatient, rehab, nursing homes, and more.

Advanced Welding

Learn to work from welding blueprints and develop skills in many different processes.

Diesel Technology

This program will get you started on a career that could have you working on big rigs, cruise ships, drilling platforms, power stations, and more.

Automotive Fundamentals & Performance Tuner

Technicians trained to work on this high-tech equipment have many career opportunities awaiting them.

Health Information Technology

Train in medical records coding, health claims examining, and medical billing.

Welcome to Western Technical College


 

Preparing students for careers is what we do every day. Our real-world approach to education is one of the many reasons Western Technical College alumni find success in their careers.

We have worked tirelessly to create an atmosphere where students can succeed and where they have a sense of belonging. In the end, our entire staff will always do what is best for you, the student.
