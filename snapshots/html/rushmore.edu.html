Welcome to Rushmore University

The Rushmore Global Graduate Degree and Professional Education

Dr. Michael Cox
Founder

In 1996, we challenged the educational establishment by creating totally flexible, real-world, online Master's and Doctoral programs that feature academic freedom and the power to control how, where, when and what you learn (view our online presentation).

Our programs are based on written papers. You tailor your own curriculum with the guidance of our renowned professors. There are no exams or required courses. We use our version of the Oxford Tutorial Method to work with you, one-on-one, to design and complete your custom program. Moreover, our editors help you improve your writing skills, whether English is your first or second language.

In 2002, the Rushmore approach to higher education was validated by Professor Jeffrey Pfeffer of the Stanford Graduate School of Business in his widely publicized article, The End of Business Schools. Based on 40 years of research, he criticized how tenured professors at traditional business schools force students to study obsolete curricula, wasting thousands of hours of their time on irrelevant courses. In his own words:

"Little of what is taught to students in business school prepares them for the corporate workplace.  You have to question what goes on in the two years it takes to get an MBA. ... One of the problems is that much of the business school curriculum has remained unchanged since the 1960s.  Business schools rely on outmoded teaching methods and do not afford students an opportunity for practical experience."

This problem exists because tenured professors at these schools have academic freedom (the power to control what, how when and where students will learn) and their students have none.  Your Rushmore degree will be a custom-made program, designed just for you with the help of your Faculty Advisor.  Our Professors will coach, mentor, tutor, advise and inspire you.  You will benefit from their experience as CEOs, corporate executives, consultants, entrepreneurs and published authors as they work with you on a one-on-one basis.

Today our programs have widespread acceptance in the international business world.  Fortune 500 corporations (including GE, AT&T, Nokia, EDS, Walt Disney and Oracle), international organizations and governments recognize the superiority of our 100% relevant and effective programs.  Explore our web site further and learn how Rushmore’s programs work and why traditional programs do not.

Michael Cox, PhD, CPA
Founder of Rushmore University
