Commencement 2012

Join us on May 5th at 9:00 AM in congratulating the 264 students graduating during the commencement exercises of Thomas University.  For the Class of 2012, Commencement marks the culmination of years of study and preparation that have gone into their degrees.  The presence of family and friends assembling on the Ben Grace Quadrangle signifies the support, love, and belief in these students that helped to make their graduation possible.  Congratulations to the Class of 2012!

 Registration for Summer semester 2012 and Fall semester 2012 is now OPEN!

Contact your advisor today!  Register early so you get the courses you need at the date/time you want!!

View Summer 2012 and Fall 2012 schedules here. For the most up-to-date Summer and Fall 2012 Schedules, please go to Hawklink.

If you are a current student returning for Summer and/or Fall, you must register before the end of this semester (May 4).

Summer books are now available for purchase a the TU E-Bookstore. 
 
Fall classes’ books can be viewed HERE.  These fall books will be offered on the TU E-Bookstore site beginning July 16th.

Information Literacy Mystery Winners!

Congratulations to Jamie Hart (online winner) and Hiram Collins (on-campus winner) pictured here with TU Library Director, Amber Brock, for being the first to solve the Information Literacy Mystery!!  They both won a new Kindle Fire.  Other prizes are still available.  Get started today.  Visit the library for more information. 

TELL US YOUR STORY!

We would love to hear your personal story of transformation as a student at TU.  Students like you inspire us with your stories of overcoming obstacles, making important life and educational discoveries and achieving high educational goals - often while working and raising a family!  If your story is chosen to share, you will receive a gift certificate to the Hawk's Nest for lunch on us and your story will appear on our website.  If you choose to remain anonymous, there is a button on the submission form to indicate this.
