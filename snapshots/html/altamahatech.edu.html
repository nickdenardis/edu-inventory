ALTAMAHA TECHNICAL COLLEGE FINDS SUCCESS WITH EXPANSION INTO THE GOLDEN ISLES

By Michael Hall, The Brunswick News

Altamaha Technical College seized an opportunity for expansion in 2009 that is paying dividends today.

When Coastal Georgia Community College became College of Coastal Georgia, a four-year baccalaureate institution, it left a void in technical and skilled trade education in Glynn County.

It did not take long for the Jesup based Altamaha Tech to fill that void.

Altamaha Technical College recognizes faithful service

 

Pictured from left are Altamaha Technical College Board Chair Randal Morris, Dean of Adult Education Sandy Williams and President Lorette Hoover. (Photo provided)

 

(April 24, 2012) – Altamaha Technical College presented Sandy Williams, Dean of Adult Education, with a Faithful Service Award for her many years of dedication to the College during the Board’s monthly meeting April 18. President Lorette Hoover presented an award of appreciation to Williams for her five years of service. Williams’ office is located on the Jesup Campus, although she oversees 15 Academic Support Centers for the College in its seven county service delivery area of Appling, Camden, Glynn, Jeff Davis, Long, McIntosh and Wayne.

To learn more about registering for one of Altamaha Technical College’s more than 80 degree, diploma and certificate programs, contact the Office of Admissions at 1-800-645-8284 or go online to www.altamahatech.edu. Scholarships and financial aid are available. Summer Semester begins May 21.
 

Office of Continuing Education offers CDL Refresher Plus HAZMAT/TWIC Certification in Baxley, Golden Isles

JESUP, Ga. (March 22, 2012)— Altamaha Technical College’s Office of Continuing Education opened registration for its Summer 2012 CDL Refresher Plus HAZMAT/TWIC Certification Course. This course is available to current CDL licensed drivers wishing to refresh their skills and/or add HAZMAT and TWIC certification. This 60 hour program begins May 21 and will be held Monday through Thursday from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. for two weeks. Registration deadline is May 8. Contact Sherry Green at 912-588-2575 to register. To learn more about the course and its requirements, contact Instructor Fallon Weyant at 912-280-4000, ext. 4415 or email him at This e-mail address is being protected from spam bots, you need JavaScript enabled to view it . This course will be offered on the Golden Isles campus.

For more information about Altamaha Technical College’s more than 80 degree, diploma and certificate programs, visit Altamaha Technical College at one of its five regional locations in Baxley, Brunswick, Hazlehurst, Jesup and Kingsland or go online to www.altamahatech.edu.
