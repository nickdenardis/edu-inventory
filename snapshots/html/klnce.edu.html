KLN College of Engineering (KLNCE) is the first self-financing Co-educational Engineering College, Established in 1994 in Sivagangai District. College is declared as Linguistic Minority Status belonging to Backward Community (Sourashtra).
Sponsored by K.L.N. Sourashtra College of Engineering Council, Madurai.

Vision:
To attain autonomous status and strive for continual improvement in quality management system.
Mission:
To promote into a premier learning center of academic excellence in Engineering, Technology and Management emphasizing research, social concern and character development.
