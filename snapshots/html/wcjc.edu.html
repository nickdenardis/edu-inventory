News and Information        
New Student Orientation

New Student Orientation is offered to all first time entering students on July 10 at the Sugar Land campus, July 11 at the Richmond campus, and July 12 at the Wharton campus. Register online now. Seating is limited. Sessions are approximately 4 hours long prepare you for academic success. Build your plan, select the right classes, and receive hands on training with WCJC's registration program. Enjoy early registration for fall courses by registering for New Student Orientation online now! Click here to sign up.
Upcoming Board Election

The election of three seats on the Wharton County Junior College Board of Trustees will be held on May 12, and WCJC district residents can cast their ballots through early voting beginning April 30. Click here to see early voting locations, dates and times.
Academic Recognition Awards

The Academic Recognition Awards will be held on Thursday, May 10 at 6:30 p.m. in the evening at the Pioneer Student Center on the Wharton campus. Departmental Awards and GPA Honors Certificates will be awarded.
